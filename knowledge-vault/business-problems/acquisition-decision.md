# Acquisition Decision

**Domain.** [[domains/m-and-a]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/m-and-a]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/synergy-valuation]]

## Problem description

A business is evaluating an acquisition target: whether to buy, at what price, and how
to integrate. The decision depends on standalone value, synergy potential, integration
feasibility, and price vs. max bid.
