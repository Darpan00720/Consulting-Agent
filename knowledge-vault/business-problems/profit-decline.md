# Profit or Margin Decline

**Domain.** [[domains/profitability]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/profitability]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/profit-tree]]

## Problem description

A business is experiencing a decline in operating margin or absolute profit.
The root cause is unknown and may be revenue-side (price erosion, volume decline, mix shift)
or cost-side (COGS inflation, operating deleverage, overhead creep).
