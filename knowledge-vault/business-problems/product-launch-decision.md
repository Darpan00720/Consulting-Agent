# New Product Launch Decision

**Domain.** [[domains/new-product-launch]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/new-product-launch]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/launch-economics-gtm]]

## Problem description

A business is evaluating whether to launch a new product: is there real customer demand,
are the unit economics viable after cannibalization, and is the GTM plan credible?
