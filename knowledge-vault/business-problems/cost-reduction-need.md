# Cost Base Reduction

**Domain.** [[domains/cost-reduction]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/cost-reduction]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/cost-decomposition-benchmarking]]

## Problem description

A business must reduce its cost base by a target amount without harming core operations.
The opportunity may lie in quick-win procurement savings, structural operating model changes,
or strategic exit from non-core activities.
