**Domain.** [[domains/private-equity-due-diligence]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/private-equity-due-diligence]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/commercial-dd-value-creation]]

## Problem description

A private equity fund is evaluating an acquisition: is the commercial thesis real,
is financial quality sound, are value-creation levers credible and specific,
and is the risk profile within the fund's investment criteria?
