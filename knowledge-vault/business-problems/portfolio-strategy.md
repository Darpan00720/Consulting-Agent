# Portfolio & Corporate Strategy

**Domain.** [[domains/corporate-strategy]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/corporate-strategy]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/playing-to-win]]

## Problem description

A business needs to define or refresh its corporate-level strategy:
where to play (markets and segments), how to win (competitive advantage),
portfolio balance across growth horizons, and capital allocation.
