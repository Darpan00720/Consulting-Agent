# Digital Transformation Investment Priorities

**Domain.** [[domains/digital-transformation]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/digital-transformation]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/digital-maturity-value-feasibility]]

## Problem description

A business needs to prioritize digital transformation investments across its value chain.
The question is where digital creates the most value, what is feasible given data and
capability state, and what operating model changes are required.
