# Customer Lifetime Value Improvement

**Domain.** [[domains/customer-strategy]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/customer-strategy]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/segmentation-clv]]

## Problem description

A business seeks to maximize customer lifetime value by improving retention,
expanding wallet share, and investing in high-value segments —
while eliminating investment in low-value or loss-making customers.
