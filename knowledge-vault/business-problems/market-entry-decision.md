# Market Entry Decision

**Domain.** [[domains/market-entry]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/market-entry]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/market-attractiveness-right-to-win]]

## Problem description

A business is evaluating whether and how to enter a new geographic or product market.
The decision requires assessing market attractiveness, right-to-win, optimal entry mode,
and entry economics.
