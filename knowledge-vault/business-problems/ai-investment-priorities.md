# AI Investment Priorities

**Domain.** [[domains/ai-strategy]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/ai-strategy]]

## Problem description

A business is deciding where to invest in AI to create the most value.
The question spans value identification, data and feasibility readiness, build/buy/partner
decisions, operating model design, and responsible-AI governance.
