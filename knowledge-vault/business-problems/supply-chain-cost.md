# Supply Chain Cost & Service Improvement

**Domain.** [[domains/supply-chain]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/supply-chain]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/scor-network-optimization]]

## Problem description

A business faces elevated supply chain costs or service level shortfalls.
Root cause may lie in planning (forecast accuracy), sourcing (procurement efficiency),
operations (OEE), delivery (fulfilment cost), or resilience (single points of failure).
