# Organizational Fit-for-Purpose

**Domain.** [[domains/organizational-design]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/organizational-design]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/operating-model-spans-layers]]

## Problem description

A business is not executing its strategy effectively and suspects organizational causes:
structural misalignment, unclear decision rights, inefficient processes, or capability gaps.
