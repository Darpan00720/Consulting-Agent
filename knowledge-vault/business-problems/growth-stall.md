# Revenue Growth Stall

**Domain.** [[domains/revenue-growth]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/revenue-growth]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/growth-driver-tree]]

## Problem description

A business has seen revenue growth slow materially below historical or market rates.
The growth stall may be driven by acquisition, retention, or expansion failures,
or by market saturation requiring new market or product moves.
