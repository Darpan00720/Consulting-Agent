# Commercial Engine Underperformance

**Domain.** [[domains/sales-and-marketing]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/sales-and-marketing]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/funnel-gtm-economics]]

## Problem description

A business is not hitting revenue or growth targets and suspects the commercial
engine — whether from funnel leakage, poor channel economics, or sales force
productivity below peer benchmarks.
