# Pricing Under-Capture

**Domain.** [[domains/pricing]]

**Issue tree.** [[issue-trees/pricing]]

**Primary framework.** [[frameworks/value-based-pricing]]

## Problem description

A business suspects it is leaving money on the table through sub-optimal pricing —
whether from price levels set below value, leakage through discounts and exceptions,
or a price structure that fails to capture heterogeneous willingness-to-pay.
