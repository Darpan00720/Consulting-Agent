# Capability & Talent Model

**Domain(s).** [[domains/organizational-design]]

**Purpose.** Map current organizational capabilities against strategy-required capabilities to identify gaps and talent development priorities.

**When to use.** When the organization lacks the capabilities to execute its strategy or has made a significant strategic pivot.

## Diagnostic questions
- Which capabilities are critical to the strategy but currently underdeveloped?
- Should missing capabilities be built internally, bought (hire/acquire), or borrowed (partner)?

## Success metrics
- Capability gap score by domain
- Critical role vacancy rate
- Succession depth at key roles

## Common risks
- Confusing headcount levels with actual capability quality

## Common mistakes
- Assessing current capability without first defining the strategy-required future-state capability profile
