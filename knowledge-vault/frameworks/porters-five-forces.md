# Porter's Five Forces

**Domain(s).** [[domains/market-entry]]

**Purpose.** Assess industry attractiveness by analyzing competitive intensity, supplier/buyer power, threat of new entrants, and substitutes.

**When to use.** When evaluating whether to enter a new market or understanding the structural profitability of an industry.

## Diagnostic questions
- How intense is rivalry among existing competitors in this market?
- Do suppliers or buyers hold structural pricing power that limits margin?
- Are barriers to entry high enough to protect long-run profitability?

## Success metrics
- Industry structural attractiveness score
- Long-run margin potential estimate

## Common risks
- Static analysis missing dynamic shifts (e.g., digitization restructuring an industry)

## Common mistakes
- Treating the five forces as equally weighted; ignoring which force is the primary profit driver
