# Capital Allocation Framework

**Domain(s).** [[domains/corporate-strategy]]

**Purpose.** Decide how to deploy free cash flow across reinvestment in the business, M&A, dividends, and share buybacks to maximize shareholder returns.

**When to use.** When setting capital allocation policy or evaluating competing investment priorities across the portfolio.

## Diagnostic questions
- What is the marginal return on reinvestment vs. returning capital to shareholders?
- Are we funding value-creating vs. value-destroying investments?

## Success metrics
- ROIC vs. WACC by business unit
- Total shareholder return (TSR) vs. peers
- Reinvestment rate vs. return on reinvestment

## Common risks
- Over-retaining cash in low-return businesses due to management attachment to assets

## Common mistakes
- Separating capital allocation decisions from where-to-play and how-to-win strategic choices
