# Sales Force Effectiveness

**Domain(s).** [[domains/sales-and-marketing]]

**Purpose.** Diagnose and improve sales productivity through pipeline analysis, quota attainment, coverage model, and sales process optimization.

**When to use.** When quota attainment is below target or sales productivity varies significantly across the team.

## Diagnostic questions
- Where in the pipeline do deals stall or drop out most frequently?
- What separates top-quartile reps from the median in win rate and deal size?

## Success metrics
- Revenue per sales FTE
- Quota attainment % (team and individual)
- Pipeline-to-close conversion rate

## Common risks
- Adding headcount to solve what is fundamentally a productivity or process problem

## Common mistakes
- Diagnosing pipeline volume without diagnosing pipeline quality and stage-to-stage conversion
