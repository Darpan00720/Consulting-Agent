# BCG Growth-Share Matrix

**Domain(s).** [[domains/revenue-growth]] · [[domains/corporate-strategy]]

**Purpose.** Plot business units or products on market growth vs. relative market share to guide portfolio investment decisions.

**When to use.** When allocating investment across multiple products, business units, or markets.

## Diagnostic questions
- Which units are Stars (high growth, high share) requiring continued investment?
- Which are Cash Cows to harvest and Dogs to divest?

## Success metrics
- Portfolio balance: ratio of Stars to Cash Cows
- Market share in high-growth markets

## Common risks
- Treating the matrix as a prescription rather than a first-cut diagnostic

## Common mistakes
- Ignoring cross-unit synergies; oversimplifying with only two dimensions
