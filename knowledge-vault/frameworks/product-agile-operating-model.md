# Product & Agile Operating Model

**Domain(s).** [[domains/digital-transformation]]

**Purpose.** Redesign the operating model around empowered product teams with embedded cross-functional capabilities and agile delivery.

**When to use.** When the organization needs to accelerate digital delivery and reduce cross-functional hand-offs.

## Diagnostic questions
- Is delivery slow because of organizational silos or excessive hand-offs?
- Are product teams empowered to make technical and roadmap decisions without escalation?

## Success metrics
- Deployment frequency
- Lead time for changes (commit to production)
- Team autonomy index

## Common risks
- Agile washing — renaming teams without changing decision rights or funding models

## Common mistakes
- Applying agile methods to stable process optimization rather than product/service development
