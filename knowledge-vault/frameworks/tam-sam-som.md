# TAM / SAM / SOM Sizing

**Domain(s).** [[domains/market-entry]]

**Purpose.** Estimate Total Addressable Market, Serviceable Addressable Market, and Serviceable Obtainable Market to size the entry opportunity.

**When to use.** When justifying entry investment or forecasting achievable revenue in a new market.

## Diagnostic questions
- What is the realistic addressable market given our go-to-market constraints?
- What share is achievable within a 3–5 year horizon given competitive dynamics?

## Success metrics
- SOM revenue estimate
- Market share trajectory by Year 3

## Common risks
- TAM inflation by using overly broad market definitions

## Common mistakes
- Presenting TAM as the relevant opportunity without SAM and SOM haircuts
