# Playing-to-Win

**Domain.** [[domains/corporate-strategy]]

**Purpose.** Make a coherent cascade of choices — aspiration, where to play, how to win, capabilities, systems — backed by capital allocation.

**When to use.** Corporate/portfolio strategy and capital allocation.
**When not to use.** Single-business operational issues.

## Logic
Winning aspiration → where to play → how to win → capabilities → management systems.

## Diagnostic questions
- Where do we have genuine advantage?
- Is the portfolio balanced?

## Success metrics
- ROIC by business unit
- Growth-horizon balance
- TSR
