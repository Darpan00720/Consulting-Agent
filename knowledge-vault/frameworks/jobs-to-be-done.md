# Jobs-to-Be-Done (JTBD)

**Domain(s).** [[domains/new-product-launch]] · [[domains/customer-strategy]]

**Purpose.** Identify the functional, social, and emotional jobs customers are trying to get done, to anchor product design and positioning on real demand.

**When to use.** When validating product-market fit, defining launch positioning, or diagnosing retention problems.

## Diagnostic questions
- What job is the customer hiring this product to do?
- What are they switching from, and what makes switching acceptable?

## Success metrics
- Customer adoption rate
- Job satisfaction score vs. prior solution

## Common risks
- Confusing features that customers want with the underlying jobs they need done

## Common mistakes
- Running JTBD research with customers who have not yet actively tried to accomplish the job
