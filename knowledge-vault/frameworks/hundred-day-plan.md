# 100-Day Plan

**Domain(s).** [[domains/private-equity-due-diligence]]

**Purpose.** Define the first 100-day post-close priorities for operational quick wins, governance setup, and value-creation plan execution.

**When to use.** Immediately post-close to accelerate value creation and stabilize the business under new ownership.

## Diagnostic questions
- Which quick wins can demonstrate early value within the first 100 days?
- Are key management retention and governance structures confirmed by Day 1?

## Success metrics
- Quick-win EBITDA impact realized in first 100 days
- Management retention rate at Day 100

## Common risks
- Disrupting the business with too many concurrent initiatives in the fragile post-close period

## Common mistakes
- Over-emphasizing cost actions in the 100-day plan at the expense of growth enablement
