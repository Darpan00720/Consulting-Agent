# Quality of Earnings (QoE)

**Domain(s).** [[domains/private-equity-due-diligence]]

**Purpose.** Adjust reported EBITDA to identify non-recurring items, one-time boosts, and accounting choices that inflate reported earnings.

**When to use.** Before finalizing acquisition price; to determine sustainable, recurring EBITDA.

## Diagnostic questions
- What is the non-recurring vs. recurring component of reported EBITDA?
- Are revenue recognition policies aggressive relative to industry norms?

## Success metrics
- Adjusted EBITDA vs. reported EBITDA gap
- Non-recurring item % of total EBITDA

## Common risks
- Management presenting optimistic one-time exclusions that inflate adjusted EBITDA

## Common mistakes
- Accepting management EBITDA adjustments without independent verification
