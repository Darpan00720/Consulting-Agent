# Operating-Model Design & Spans-and-Layers

**Domain.** [[domains/organizational-design]]

**Purpose.** Align structure, process, and decision rights to strategy; right-size spans and layers.

**When to use.** Redesigning an operating model or org structure.
**When not to use.** When the issue is strategy or capability, not structure.

## Logic
Strategy → structure → process → decision rights → people/capability. Structure follows strategy and decisions.

## Diagnostic questions
- Are there too many layers?
- Are decision rights unclear?

## Success metrics
- Span of control
- Number of layers
- Decision cycle time
