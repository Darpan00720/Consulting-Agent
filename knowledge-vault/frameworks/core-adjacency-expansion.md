# Core & Adjacency Expansion

**Domain(s).** [[domains/corporate-strategy]]

**Purpose.** Grow from a strong core business into adjacent markets or capabilities using shared assets, while avoiding diversification traps.

**When to use.** When the core business has strong economics and growth requires adjacency moves rather than pure market-share gains.

## Diagnostic questions
- Does the adjacency genuinely leverage existing capabilities, assets, or customer relationships?
- What is our competitive advantage in the adjacency?

## Success metrics
- Adjacency revenue as % of total
- ROIC in adjacency vs. core ROIC

## Common risks
- Overestimating the transferability of core capabilities to adjacent markets

## Common mistakes
- Defining adjacencies too broadly, straying into unfamiliar territory without a clear right-to-win
