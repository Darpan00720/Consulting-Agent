# Customer Journey Mapping

**Domain(s).** [[domains/customer-strategy]]

**Purpose.** Visualize the end-to-end customer experience across touchpoints to identify pain points, moments of truth, and improvement opportunities.

**When to use.** When diagnosing experience-driven churn or designing service and product improvements.

## Diagnostic questions
- Where in the journey do customers experience the most friction?
- Which moments of truth drive or destroy customer loyalty?

## Success metrics
- Journey satisfaction score by stage
- Churn rate by journey drop-off point

## Common risks
- Mapping the designed (intended) journey rather than the actual experienced journey

## Common mistakes
- Creating journey maps without linking findings to specific business or design actions
