# Exit Analysis

**Domain(s).** [[domains/private-equity-due-diligence]]

**Purpose.** Evaluate exit options (strategic sale, secondary buyout, IPO), timing, and achievable multiple at exit to determine investment return.

**When to use.** When scoping the investment thesis at entry or when approaching the exit horizon.

## Diagnostic questions
- Who are the most likely exit buyers and what multiples do they typically pay?
- Which EBITDA improvements or growth vectors maximally expand the exit multiple?

## Success metrics
- Expected exit multiple (EV/EBITDA)
- IRR and MOIC at target exit scenario

## Common risks
- Exit timing risk — multiple compression if market conditions deteriorate at exit

## Common mistakes
- Underwriting the deal on a peak-market multiple exit assumption
