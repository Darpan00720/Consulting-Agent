# Market & Competitive Analysis (DD)

**Domain(s).** [[domains/private-equity-due-diligence]]

**Purpose.** Assess the target company's competitive position, market share trends, and durability of competitive advantage as part of commercial due diligence.

**When to use.** When validating the commercial growth thesis in an acquisition.

## Diagnostic questions
- Is the target's market share growing or declining on a trend basis?
- Are competitive moats (switching costs, network effects, scale advantages) durable?

## Success metrics
- Market share trend (3–5 year CAGR)
- Competitive position score vs. peer set

## Common risks
- Management's own market definition inflating perceived market share

## Common mistakes
- Relying on management's competitive assessments without third-party triangulation
