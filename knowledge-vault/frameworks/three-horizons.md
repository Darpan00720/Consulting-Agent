# Three Horizons of Growth

**Domain(s).** [[domains/corporate-strategy]]

**Purpose.** Allocate strategic investment across three time horizons: defending/extending the core (H1), building emerging businesses (H2), and creating future options (H3).

**When to use.** When setting portfolio strategy and ensuring the organization invests sufficiently in future growth.

## Diagnostic questions
- Is investment balanced across all three horizons relative to strategy?
- Are H2 and H3 initiatives receiving sufficient resources to mature?

## Success metrics
- Investment % by horizon vs. strategic target
- H2/H3 pipeline revenue as % of projected 5-year revenue

## Common risks
- Core business pressures systematically cannibalizing H2 and H3 investment

## Common mistakes
- Applying short-term financial return metrics to H3 option-stage investments
