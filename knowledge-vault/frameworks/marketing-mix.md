# Marketing Mix (4Ps / 7Ps)

**Domain(s).** [[domains/sales-and-marketing]]

**Purpose.** Analyze Product, Price, Place, and Promotion (+ People, Process, Physical Evidence) to identify commercial levers and alignment with customer needs.

**When to use.** When diagnosing commercial underperformance or designing a new go-to-market strategy.

## Diagnostic questions
- Which P is most out of alignment with customer needs or competitive positioning?
- Is the promotional mix matched to where the target customer is in their buying journey?

## Success metrics
- Market share
- Funnel conversion rate
- Brand awareness (prompted and unprompted)

## Common risks
- Treating the 4Ps as independent levers when they are mutually reinforcing

## Common mistakes
- Focusing heavily on promotion while neglecting underlying price or product-fit issues
