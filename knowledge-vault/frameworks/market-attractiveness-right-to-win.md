# Market Attractiveness × Right-to-Win

**Domain.** [[domains/market-entry]]

**Purpose.** Test attractiveness, right-to-win, and entry economics: attractive AND winnable AND affordable.

**When to use.** Evaluating a market-entry go/no-go and entry mode.
**When not to use.** Core-business fix-first situations.

## Logic
Attractiveness (size·growth·intensity) → right-to-win → mode (build/buy/ally) → economics.

## Diagnostic questions
- Is the market big or genuinely attractive?
- Do we have a right to win?
- What is the retaliation risk?

## Success metrics
- SOM
- Payback period
- IRR
