# Cohort & Retention Analysis

**Domain(s).** [[domains/revenue-growth]]

**Purpose.** Track customer cohorts over time to identify retention trends, churn patterns, and expansion revenue.

**When to use.** When growth is stalling and the mix of acquisition vs. retention effects is unclear.

## Diagnostic questions
- Are cohorts retained at similar rates across vintages?
- Does revenue expand within retained cohorts over time?

## Success metrics
- Cohort retention rate
- Net revenue retention (NRR)

## Common risks
- Confusing logo retention with revenue retention

## Common mistakes
- Averaging churn across cohorts with very different behaviors and risk profiles
