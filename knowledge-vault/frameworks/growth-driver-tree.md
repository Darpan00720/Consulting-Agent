# Growth Driver Tree

**Domain.** [[domains/revenue-growth]]

**Purpose.** Map growth across new vs existing customers, volume, price/mix, and new markets/products.

**When to use.** Diagnosing stalled growth and prioritizing initiatives.
**When not to use.** When the real problem is cost or margin, not growth.

## Logic
New vs existing customers × volume × price/mix; new markets/products. Diagnose where growth stalled before adding levers.

## Diagnostic questions
- Is it an acquisition, retention, or expansion problem?
- Is it a market decline or a share loss?

## Success metrics
- Revenue growth %
- Net revenue retention
- Market share
