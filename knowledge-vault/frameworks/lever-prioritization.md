# Cost Lever Prioritization

**Domain(s).** [[domains/cost-reduction]]

**Purpose.** Rank cost reduction opportunities by savings potential, feasibility, implementation cost, and risk to prioritize action.

**When to use.** After decomposing the cost base and identifying candidate levers, before committing to implementation.

## Diagnostic questions
- Which levers deliver the largest savings with the least operational risk?
- What is the net savings after implementation costs and restructuring charges?

## Success metrics
- Expected run-rate savings (annualized)
- Net present value of savings net of implementation cost

## Common risks
- Selecting easy levers that collectively deliver insufficient savings

## Common mistakes
- Ignoring implementation cost and one-time restructuring charges in the business case
