# Segmentation & CLV

**Domain.** [[domains/customer-strategy]]

**Purpose.** Segment customers, value each segment by CLV, and invest by segment value.

**When to use.** Segmentation, retention, and experience strategy.
**When not to use.** When product or market problems sit upstream of customers.

## Logic
Segments → value (CLV) per segment → journey/experience gaps → retention/expansion levers.

## Diagnostic questions
- Which segments create value?
- Where does the customer journey break?

## Success metrics
- CLV
- Churn
- NPS
- Net revenue retention
