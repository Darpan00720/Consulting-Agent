# RACI & Decision Rights

**Domain(s).** [[domains/organizational-design]]

**Purpose.** Define who is Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed for each key decision to clarify ownership and eliminate ambiguity.

**When to use.** When decisions are slow, escalated unnecessarily, or reversed frequently due to unclear ownership.

## Diagnostic questions
- Which key decisions have no single clearly accountable owner?
- Which decisions require too many consultations before resolution, slowing execution?

## Success metrics
- Decision cycle time (days)
- Escalation rate
- Decision reversal rate

## Common risks
- RACI becoming a bureaucratic compliance exercise rather than enabling faster, clearer decisions

## Common mistakes
- Assigning multiple people as Accountable for the same decision, defeating the purpose
