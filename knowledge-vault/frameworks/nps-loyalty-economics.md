# NPS & Loyalty Economics

**Domain(s).** [[domains/customer-strategy]]

**Purpose.** Measure customer loyalty via Net Promoter Score and link NPS cohorts to revenue outcomes to build the business case for experience investment.

**When to use.** When quantifying the financial value of improving customer experience.

## Diagnostic questions
- Do Promoters spend more and churn less than Detractors in our customer base?
- What is the revenue-per-point-of-NPS linkage in our specific business?

## Success metrics
- NPS score (absolute and trend)
- Revenue and churn delta between Promoter and Detractor cohorts

## Common risks
- Managing NPS as a target rather than as a diagnostic leading indicator

## Common mistakes
- Benchmarking NPS without controlling for industry and exact survey methodology differences
