# Zero-Based Budgeting (ZBB)

**Domain(s).** [[domains/cost-reduction]]

**Purpose.** Build budgets from zero each period by justifying every cost item, rather than adjusting prior-year actuals.

**When to use.** When the cost structure has accumulated excess over many incremental budget cycles.

## Diagnostic questions
- Can each cost item be justified by current business need and strategy?
- Are costs driven by historical precedent rather than current value delivered?

## Success metrics
- Run-rate cost savings vs. prior baseline
- SG&A as % of revenue

## Common risks
- Destroying capability needed for growth by cutting too deep

## Common mistakes
- Using ZBB as a one-time exercise rather than a sustained budgeting discipline
