# Share-of-Wallet Analysis

**Domain(s).** [[domains/revenue-growth]]

**Purpose.** Estimate how much of a customer's total category spend the firm captures, and identify expansion opportunities.

**When to use.** When existing customers represent the primary growth opportunity.

## Diagnostic questions
- What share of customer category spend do we currently capture?
- Which product or service lines are most under-penetrated in the base?

## Success metrics
- Share of wallet %
- Cross-sell attach rate

## Common risks
- Overestimating the total customer spend denominator

## Common mistakes
- Treating all share-of-wallet gains as equally profitable regardless of margin
