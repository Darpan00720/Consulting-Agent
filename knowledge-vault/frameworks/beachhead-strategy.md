# Beachhead Market Strategy

**Domain(s).** [[domains/market-entry]]

**Purpose.** Identify and dominate a narrow initial market segment before expanding, reducing entry risk and capital required.

**When to use.** When entering a large market against established players where full-market entry is not feasible.

## Diagnostic questions
- Which narrow segment can we dominate first with current capabilities and resources?
- Does early beachhead dominance create a credible platform for subsequent expansion?

## Success metrics
- Beachhead segment market share
- Time to profitability in beachhead segment

## Common risks
- Becoming trapped in the beachhead without a credible expansion path

## Common mistakes
- Choosing a beachhead for ease of winning rather than for strategic leverage toward adjacent segments
