# Funnel/Conversion & GTM Economics

**Domain.** [[domains/sales-and-marketing]]

**Purpose.** Diagnose funnel conversion and channel economics; fix the biggest leak and fund the best LTV:CAC channels.

**When to use.** Diagnosing funnel leaks and channel economics.
**When not to use.** When product or pricing is the true constraint.

## Logic
Funnel stages × conversion × CAC/LTV by channel.

## Diagnostic questions
- Where does the funnel leak?
- What are the channel economics?

## Success metrics
- CAC
- LTV:CAC
- Conversion rate
- Sales productivity
