# Commercial DD & Value-Creation Plan

**Domain.** [[domains/private-equity-due-diligence]]

**Purpose.** Test the investment thesis, size the upside, and price the risk across a commercial due-diligence lens.

**When to use.** Commercial due diligence for a transaction.
**When not to use.** Outside a transaction context.

## Logic
Market attractiveness → competitive position → growth-thesis validity → value-creation levers → risks → exit.

## Diagnostic questions
- Is the investment thesis real?
- What is the quality of earnings?
- What are the upside levers?

## Success metrics
- Entry/exit multiple
- EBITDA growth
- IRR/MOIC
