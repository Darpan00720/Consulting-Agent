# Post-Merger Integration (PMI)

**Domain(s).** [[domains/m-and-a]]

**Purpose.** Plan and execute the integration of an acquired entity to capture synergies while managing business continuity risk.

**When to use.** After deal close, to realize the synergies that justified the acquisition price.

## Diagnostic questions
- Which synergies require active integration to realize (vs. passive)?
- What is the integration risk to business continuity in each function?

## Success metrics
- Synergy capture % vs. plan
- Integration cost vs. budget
- Employee retention rate at Day 100

## Common risks
- Cultural clash destroying value faster than synergies are captured

## Common mistakes
- Under-resourcing the integration team and treating integration as part-time alongside day jobs
