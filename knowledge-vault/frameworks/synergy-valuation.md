# Synergy Valuation & Deal Rationale

**Domain.** [[domains/m-and-a]]

**Purpose.** Value the target standalone plus risk-adjusted synergies; pay no more than that.

**When to use.** Evaluating an acquisition or divestiture.
**When not to use.** When organic growth or a partnership clearly dominates.

## Logic
Rationale → standalone value → synergies (rev + cost − dis-synergies − integration cost) → price vs max → integration risk → alternatives.

## Diagnostic questions
- Why this target, why now?
- Are the synergies real and risk-adjusted?

## Success metrics
- Synergy capture %
- ROIC vs WACC
- Accretion/dilution
