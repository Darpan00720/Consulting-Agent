# Technology

**Structure.** Software, hardware, and platform businesses with scalable unit economics,
high R&D intensity, and network-effect driven competitive dynamics. [Inference from ADR-004 §6]

**Key drivers.** Product velocity, network effects, talent retention in a competitive market.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Common challenges.** Customer retention at scale; replicating unit economics as markets
mature; talent competition. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical KPIs.** ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue — sector-specific), NRR (Net Revenue
Retention — sector-specific), [[kpis/cac|CAC]] and [[kpis/ltv|LTV]] tracked via
[[kpis/ltv-cac-ratio|LTV:CAC ratio]], [[kpis/customer-churn|Customer Churn]].
ARR and NRR benchmarks are reviewer-supplied (D-6 Hybrid policy). [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Regulatory context.** Data privacy requirements (GDPR, CCPA), antitrust scrutiny for
large platform businesses. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical engagements.** Growth strategy, pricing optimisation, AI strategy, go-to-market
design. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]
