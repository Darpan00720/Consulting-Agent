# Consumer Goods

**Structure.** Branded product manufacturers selling through retail and direct channels
with significant trade investment and brand marketing spend. [Inference from ADR-004 §6]

**Key drivers.** Brand equity, distribution breadth and depth, trade spend efficiency.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Common challenges.** Private-label competition on price; retailer channel power affecting
margin negotiation and shelf access. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical KPIs.** [[kpis/gross-margin|Gross Margin]], trade spend as a percentage of
revenue (sector-specific), [[kpis/market-share|Market Share]]. Trade spend benchmarks are
reviewer-supplied (D-6 Hybrid policy). [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Regulatory context.** Product labelling requirements, ingredient and safety standards,
advertising regulations. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical engagements.** Pricing strategy, growth strategy, supply chain optimisation.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]
