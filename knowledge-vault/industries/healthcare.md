# Healthcare

**Structure.** Healthcare delivery, pharmaceutical, and medical device organisations
governed by payer contracts and clinical regulatory frameworks. [Inference from ADR-004 §6]

**Key drivers.** Demographic demand growth, reimbursement rates, clinical outcome performance.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Common challenges.** Cost inflation driven by labour and technology; access disparities
across geographies and payer types. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical KPIs.** Cost per episode, utilisation rates, clinical outcomes — all sector-
specific and not in the canonical 14-KPI catalog. Benchmark values are reviewer-supplied
(D-6 Hybrid policy). [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Regulatory context.** Heavy: HIPAA data privacy, payer contract compliance, FDA approval
pathways. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical engagements.** Cost reduction, operations improvement, digital health transformation.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]
