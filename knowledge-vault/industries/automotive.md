# Automotive

**Structure.** Vehicle manufacturers, tier-1 and tier-2 component suppliers, and dealer
networks with high capital intensity and cyclical end-market demand. [Inference from ADR-004 §6]

**Key drivers.** Sales volume across product and geographic segments, capital expenditure
cycle discipline, rate of electrification adoption. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Common challenges.** Volume cyclicality tied to consumer confidence and credit availability;
electric vehicle transition requiring simultaneous investment in new platforms while managing
legacy combustion assets. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical KPIs.** Unit volume (sector-specific), contribution margin per unit (sector-
specific), capex intensity ratio (sector-specific) — none map directly to the canonical
14-KPI catalog. Benchmark values are reviewer-supplied (D-6 Hybrid policy). [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Regulatory context.** Emissions standards, vehicle safety regulations, supply chain
compliance requirements. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical engagements.** Cost reduction, supply chain optimisation, corporate strategy.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]
