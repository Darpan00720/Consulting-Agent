# Energy

**Structure.** Businesses extracting, generating, transmitting, or supplying energy across
fossil fuel and renewable segments. [Inference from ADR-004 §6]

**Key drivers.** Commodity price cycles, capital expenditure efficiency, pace of energy
transition. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Common challenges.** Revenue volatility from commodity exposure; decarbonisation pressure
affecting long-term asset valuations. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical KPIs.** LCOE (Levelised Cost of Energy — sector-specific), capacity factor
(sector-specific), [[kpis/roic|ROIC]]. LCOE and capacity factor benchmarks are reviewer-
supplied (D-6 Hybrid policy). [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Regulatory context.** Heavy: environmental regulations, grid access and dispatch rules,
emissions standards. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical engagements.** Corporate strategy, energy transition planning. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]
