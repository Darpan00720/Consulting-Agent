# Manufacturing

**Structure.** Industrial businesses converting raw materials into finished goods through
capital-intensive production processes. [Inference from ADR-004 §6]

**Key drivers.** Capacity utilisation, input material costs, automation adoption rate.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Common challenges.** Revenue cyclicality linked to capital-expenditure cycles; supply
chain disruption risk from concentrated sourcing. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical KPIs.** OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness — sector-specific), unit production
cost (sector-specific), [[kpis/inventory-turnover|Inventory Turnover]]. OEE and unit cost
benchmarks are reviewer-supplied (D-6 Hybrid policy). [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Regulatory context.** Safety regulations, environmental compliance requirements.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical engagements.** Cost reduction, supply chain optimisation, facility footprint
rationalisation. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]
