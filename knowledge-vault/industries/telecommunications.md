# Telecommunications

**Structure.** Network operators providing voice, data, and connectivity services through
owned or leased infrastructure with high capital intensity. [Inference from ADR-004 §6]

**Key drivers.** Network capital investment efficiency, ARPU (Average Revenue Per User),
churn management. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Common challenges.** Market saturation in developed geographies; high capital intensity;
regulatory cost constraints. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical KPIs.** ARPU (sector-specific), [[kpis/customer-churn|Customer Churn]],
capex-to-sales ratio (sector-specific). ARPU benchmarks are reviewer-supplied (D-6 Hybrid
policy). [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Regulatory context.** Heavy: spectrum licensing, roaming regulation, consumer protection
requirements. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical engagements.** Cost reduction, customer strategy, pricing optimisation.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]
