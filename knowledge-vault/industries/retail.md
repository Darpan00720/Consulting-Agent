# Retail

**Structure.** Consumer-facing businesses selling physical goods through stores, e-commerce,
or omnichannel formats. [Inference from ADR-004 §6]

**Key drivers.** Foot traffic volume, average basket size, omnichannel integration.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Common challenges.** Thin operating margins; structural volume shift to e-commerce
competition. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical KPIs.** Same-store sales growth (retail-specific; not in canonical 14-KPI catalog),
[[kpis/gross-margin|Gross Margin]], [[kpis/inventory-turnover|Inventory Turnover]].
Same-store sales benchmarks and Gross Margin benchmarks are reviewer-supplied (D-6 Hybrid
policy). [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Regulatory context.** Consumer protection legislation, labour law compliance.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical engagements.** Profitability improvement, pricing strategy, supply chain
optimisation. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]
