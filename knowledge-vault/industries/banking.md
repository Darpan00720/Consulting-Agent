# Banking

**Structure.** Financial intermediaries taking deposits, extending credit, and providing
payment services subject to capital adequacy requirements. [Inference from ADR-004 §6]

**Key drivers.** Net interest margin (NIM), credit risk management quality, customer trust.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Common challenges.** Interest rate cycle sensitivity, fintech competitive pressure,
growing compliance burden. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical KPIs.** Net Interest Margin (NIM), cost-income ratio, Non-Performing Loan (NPL)
ratio, Return on Equity (ROE) — all sector-specific and not in the canonical 14-KPI catalog.
Benchmark values are reviewer-supplied (D-6 Hybrid policy). [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Regulatory context.** Very heavy: capital adequacy (Basel III/IV), Anti-Money Laundering
(AML), Know Your Customer (KYC), market conduct regulation. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical engagements.** Digital transformation, cost reduction, customer strategy.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]
