# Insurance

**Structure.** Risk pooling businesses underwriting policies, collecting premiums, and
managing claims across life, property, casualty, and specialty lines. [Inference from ADR-004 §6]

**Key drivers.** Underwriting discipline, claims experience management, investment income
from float. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Common challenges.** Loss ratio volatility from catastrophe events; digital disruption
of underwriting and distribution models. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical KPIs.** Combined ratio, loss ratio — both sector-specific and not in the canonical
14-KPI catalog. Benchmark values are reviewer-supplied (D-6 Hybrid policy). [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Regulatory context.** Heavy: solvency capital requirements, market conduct and consumer
protection regulation, pricing adequacy oversight. [Verified: ADR-004 §6]

**Typical engagements.** Pricing optimisation, digital transformation, cost reduction.
[Verified: ADR-004 §6]
