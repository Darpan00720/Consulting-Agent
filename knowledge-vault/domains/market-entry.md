# Market Entry

**Purpose.** Decide whether and how to enter a market.

## Primary framework
- [[frameworks/market-attractiveness-right-to-win]] — Market Attractiveness × Right-to-Win

## Issue-tree logic
Attractiveness (size·growth·intensity) → right-to-win → mode (build/buy/ally) → economics.

## Diagnostic questions
- Is the market big or genuinely attractive?
- Do we have a right to win?
- What is the retaliation risk?
