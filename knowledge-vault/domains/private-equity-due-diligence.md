# Private Equity Due Diligence

**Purpose.** Test a deal thesis and value-creation plan.

## Primary framework
- [[frameworks/commercial-dd-value-creation]] — Commercial DD & Value-Creation Plan

## Issue-tree logic
Market attractiveness → competitive position → growth-thesis validity → value-creation levers → risks → exit.

## Diagnostic questions
- Is the investment thesis real?
- What is the quality of earnings?
- What are the upside levers?
