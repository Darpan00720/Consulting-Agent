# Revenue Growth

**Purpose.** Find and prioritize growth levers.

## Primary framework
- [[frameworks/growth-driver-tree]] — Growth Driver Tree

## Issue-tree logic
New vs existing customers × volume × price/mix; new markets/products. Diagnose where growth stalled before adding levers.

## Diagnostic questions
- Is it an acquisition, retention, or expansion problem?
- Is it a market decline or a share loss?
