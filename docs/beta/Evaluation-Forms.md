# StratAgent Beta — Evaluation Forms

Three instruments: a **post-engagement micro-survey** (after every engagement), a
**weekly pulse**, and an **end-of-program survey** + **interview guide**. Likert
items are 1–7 (1 = strongly disagree / worst, 7 = strongly agree / best) unless
noted. Keep every free-text box optional but encouraged.

---

## A. Post-engagement micro-survey (~5 min, after each engagement)

**Context**
1. Engagement ID / archetype: ____  · Complexity tier (A1/A2/A3): ____
2. Guided benchmark or BYO? ____
3. Did you supply real data in the prompt? (none / some / mostly)

**Outcome (Likert 1–7)**
4. The recommendation addressed my **real question**.
5. The recommendation was **useful** — I could act on it (after review).
6. The report was **clear** and well-organized.
7. I could **trace** each claim to an assumption or evidence item.
8. The **evidence/assumptions** were of acceptable quality for a first draft.
9. The **Challenger** section improved my thinking / caught something real.
10. Overall, I **trust** this output as a starting point.

**Behavioral (numeric / categorical)**
11. Time to first usable recommendation (minutes): ____
12. Would you **act** on it after review? (yes / yes-with-edits / no)
13. How many quantitative claims did you **verify or override**? ____ of ____
14. Did you **override** the core recommendation? (kept / modified / rejected)
15. Estimated **editing effort** to make it client-ready: (none / light / moderate / heavy / rewrite)
16. Did the engagement **complete** end-to-end with a report? (yes / no — where did it fail?)

**Free text**
17. What did the analysis **miss** that you know matters?
18. Strongest part of this engagement?
19. Weakest part / where did it go wrong?
20. Any **workflow friction** (confusing step, too slow, unclear output)?

---

## B. Weekly pulse (~2 min)

1. Engagements run this week: ____
2. Net usefulness so far (1–7): ____
3. One thing that worked. ____
4. One thing that frustrated you. ____
5. Are you still likely to keep using it? (yes / unsure / no) — why?

---

## C. End-of-program survey (~15 min)

**Overall quality (1–7)**
1. Problem framing · 2. Framework fit · 3. Structure (MECE) · 4. Evidence/
assumptions · 5. Strategic reasoning · 6. Financial/quant reasoning · 7.
Operational reasoning · 8. Risk analysis · 9. Recommendation quality · 10.
Implementation roadmap · 11. Report clarity · 12. Governance (Reviewer/Challenger)
value · 13. Confidence calibration (did stated confidence match reality?) · 14.
Overall consulting quality.

**Trust & adoption**
15. I trust StratAgent output enough to use it in my real work (with review). (1–7)
16. Compared with doing it **yourself unaided**, StratAgent made you: (much slower
… much faster) and (much worse … much better) — two 1–7 scales.
17. Compared with a **single-shot AI answer** (ChatGPT/Claude one prompt),
StratAgent was: (worse … better) (1–7). Why?
18. **Net Promoter:** How likely to recommend to a colleague? (0–10)
19. Would you **pay** for this? (no / maybe / yes — at what rough price point?)

**Diagnosis (free text)**
20. Top 3 strengths.
21. Top 3 weaknesses.
22. What reasoning or capability was **missing** most often?
23. What would most increase your **trust**?
24. Biggest **workflow** improvement you want.
25. When did you **override** the system, and why (pattern)?

---

## D. Semi-structured interview guide (30 min, selected participants)

**Warm-up (5 min)**
- Walk me through the last engagement you ran, start to finish.
- What did you do with the output afterward?

**Trust & usefulness (10 min)**
- Where did you trust it, and where did you not? What drove the difference?
- The report labels numbers as assumptions — did you check them? Did the labeling
  help or get in the way?
- Did the Challenger section ever change your mind? Example?
- Tell me about a time it was **wrong** or missed something. How did you notice?

**Workflow & friction (8 min)**
- Where did the process feel slow, confusing, or heavy?
- How did the wait time (minutes+) affect how you used it?
- What did you have to do that you wish the tool did?

**Value & adoption (5 min)**
- What job is StratAgent best for? What is it clearly *not* for?
- Would this replace, augment, or not fit your current workflow?
- If we fixed one thing before launch, what should it be?

**Close (2 min)**
- Anything I didn't ask that I should have?

Interviewer notes to capture: strengths, weaknesses, missing reasoning, trust
drivers, confidence mismatches, workflow friction, desired improvements, verbatim
quotes.
